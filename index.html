INTRODUCION

Seare.com.pk is a Online Tailoring Service that will break the geographical barriers and bring the whole process into a quick and easy way to access tailors. It will automate the traditional tailoring system into a modern computerized system. This will enhance data retrieval, storage and security. It is also cost effective since it will cut down on travelling cost to get your measurements taken and also going to check if your clothes has been made and is ready for collection. The clients can access their online tailors 24/7 and at any location provided they are connected to the internet. Due to the advancement in telecommunication e.g. undersea cabling, internet. We have two part of the application one is for tailor side and other is for customers.
